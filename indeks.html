My Project

Project

BETTER TYPE


Created using C# with Unity as the tool, assets designed in Adobe Illustrator, some from Freepik and Font Awesome. This mini-game aims to enhance typing speed with language options: Indonesian and English. Players type falling words before they reach the bottom line, signaling game over.

GAMALI


Developed using C# and Unity, with assets created in Adobe Illustrator and sourced from Freepik. In this game, players drive a car by pressing the space bar to start. They encounter traffic sign-based questions, testing their knowledge of traffic rules while navigating.
